About

It is started 2 years ago as AdBak bakery shop by Aditya.

The chefs of the bakery are committed to use best quality ingredients to bake the prodcuts.

Our team of bakers and pastry chefs has years of experience creating delicious baked goods. Head baker John Smith has been with us since the beginning and brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to our kitchen.

We're proud to be an active member of our local community. We participate in charity events and support local organizations, such as the Food Bank and the Farmers' Market.
